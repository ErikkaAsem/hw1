What's Capusule Cooking?

For busy professionals who must save time and want to save money, Capsule Cooking is a new meal prep service that reduces time in the kitchen to just two hours per week.

Unlike current meal prep apps, Capsule Cooking offers curated capsule recipes that cross-utilize ingredients between recipes, minimizing food waste and time spent prepping and cooking.Â 

What's Capusule Cooking?

Capsules are collections of recipes that are deigned to be cooked simultaneously

By curating capsule recipes that can be prepped and cooked simultaneously, FreshPrep aims to give people back that one resource we all wish would not slip by so quickly: time.

Why create a profile?

Create a Capsule Cooking Profile to:
  1. Receive one suggested capsule based on your history, preferences, and ratings
  2. Import your capsule ingredients into your prefered grocery delivery service
  3. Be the first to get access to new recipes
